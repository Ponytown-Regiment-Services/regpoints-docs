## Add members

Members can join your regiment in two ways: through the **Discord bot** using the `!enlist` command, or **manually** through the RegPoints web dashboard. Both methods create a member record and link it to your regiment.

---

### Via Discord - `/enlist`

The `/enlist` command lets new recruits add themselves to your regiment directly from your Discord server. It automatically records their name, starting rank, and links their Discord account.

:::tip
Refer to the [Bot Management - Enlisting](../bot_management/enlisting) guide for detailed instructions on how to set up and use the `/enlist` command.
:::

#### How a recruit uses `/enlist`

Once open, a recruit runs the command in the appropriate channel:

```
/enlist
```
![Example of the enlist command in Discord](/imgs/enlisting/command-enlist.png)

The bot will guide them through the enlistment process interactively, asking for their in-game name and other details.
![Example of the enlistment process in Discord](/imgs/enlisting/process-enlist.png)

Upon completion:

- They are added to the regiment on RegPoints.
- Their Discord account is automatically linked to their member record.
- If configured, they receive a role and the report channel is notified.

---

### Manually - Web Dashboard

If you need to add a member directly (without Discord), use the **New Member** page in the RegPoints dashboard.

<Steps>

1. Go to the 'New Member' category on the sidebar: 
![New Member page in RegPoints dashboard](/imgs/enlisting/new-member/000.png)

2. **Enter the member's name**

   Type the member's in-game name **without their rank**.
   ![New Member form - Step 1](/imgs/enlisting/new-member/001.png)

   :::note
   Maximum 50 characters. The name must not already exist in the regiment.
   :::

3. **Select a starting rank**

   Choose from the available starting ranks in the dropdown. Only ranks that are:
   - marked as the **default rank**, or
   - set to require **0 points**

   will appear as options. MWD (K9) ranks appear separately if your regiment has MWD enabled.

4. **Submit**

   Click **Submit**. If successful, you'll see a confirmation link to the new member's profile.

</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

**Member already exists:** ⇾
   Both methods will reject the submission if a member with that exact name already exists in your regiment. Check the member list before adding.


**No ranks available in dropdown** ⇾
   If the starting rank dropdown on the manual form is empty, no ranks are set as default or have 0 points. Update your rank settings in the **Manage Regiment** page under the **Ranks** tab.


**Bot can't assign the role:** ⇾
   The bot's own Discord role must be positioned **above** the recruit role in your server's role list. Drag it higher in **Server Settings ⇾ Roles**.
