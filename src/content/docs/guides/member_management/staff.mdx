You can add your staff members to the dashboard, so they can also log patrols, add or edit members, etc…

:::tip
Before beginning, you should double check if you (owner) gave the right permissions in the "role tab" (look at [Ranks & Specialties Tabs — Promotions and Titles](/guides/manage-regiment/#ranks--specialties-tabs--promotions-and-titles))
All person who will at least have the "portal access" is eligible to get access.
![Discord authorization page in French](/imgs/000_invitesManagement.png)
:::

## Permissions

You dispose of 3 'permission levels' for your staff members:

| Permissions | Access | Admin | Co-owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Dashboard access** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **View members** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Edit members** | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Delete members** | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **View patrols** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Add patrols** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Approve patrols** | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Manage regiment** | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

:::caution
Only give co-owner permissions to ***trusted staff members***, as they have full access to the regiment dashboard.
:::

## Inviting Staff Members

<Steps>
1. Go to the "Invites & Access" category on the sidebar.
    ![Discord authorization page in French](/imgs/001_invitesManagement.png)

2. This will appear:
    ![Discord authorization page in French](/imgs/002_invitesManagement.png)

3. Once you have setup the permissions for each staff member, you can invite them by sending them the invite link attributed.
    ![Discord authorization page in French](/imgs/003_invitesManagement.png)
</Steps>




:::note
They do not need to make multiple accounts if they already have one, they just need to use the same account to accept the invite and they will have access to the regiment dashboard with the permissions you set.
:::
